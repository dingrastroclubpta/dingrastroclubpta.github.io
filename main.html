What is this Website?

This is a website that aims to simplify astronomy and astrophysics. I have posted a series of articles that contain general knowledge about astronomy, such as what a star or galaxy is. Additionally, each week I post an article detailing the most interesting recent research.

I also post my astrophotography, along with a short description of the object and acquisition details.
